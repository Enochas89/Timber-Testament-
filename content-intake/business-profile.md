# Business Profile

## Business Name
Timber & Testament Custom Carpentry

## Tagline
Custom carpentry built with craftsmanship, character, and care.

## Short Description
Timber & Testament Custom Carpentry provides custom woodwork and finish carpentry for homeowners who want functional, high-quality features built specifically for their space. We focus on clean design, durable construction, and detail-driven work that adds both beauty and value to the home.

## Full Description
Timber & Testament Custom Carpentry is a custom carpentry business offering handcrafted woodwork and built-in features tailored to each client's needs. We specialize in creating custom furniture, custom cabinets, floating shelves, media walls, mantels, and other finish carpentry elements that transform ordinary spaces into something distinctive and lasting.

Our work is centered on craftsmanship, thoughtful design, and attention to detail. Whether a client needs a statement piece for a living room, added storage with custom built-ins, or a fully tailored cabinet solution, we aim to deliver work that is both visually strong and highly functional. Each project is approached with a focus on quality materials, solid construction, and a finished look that fits the home naturally.
