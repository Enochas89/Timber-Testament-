# Project 6
## Project Title
Custom Accent Wall and Finish Carpentry Upgrade

## Service Type
Finish Carpentry

## Location
Collegedale, TN

## Summary
Installed a custom accent wall with finish carpentry details to add depth, texture, and architectural interest to the room.

## Scope of Work
- Planned wall layout and pattern
- Installed trim or panel components
- Filled, sanded, and prepped surfaces
- Completed final fit and finish work
- Prepared wall for paint or final finish

## Customer Goal
The client wanted to turn a plain wall into a standout visual feature with custom character.

## Key Features
- Decorative wall treatment
- Custom layout
- Clean finish details
- Strong visual impact

## Materials
- Trim boards or panel materials
- Adhesives and fasteners
- Caulk/filler
- Paint-ready finish materials

## Outcome
The room gained a more custom, finished appearance with a standout feature wall that elevated the overall design.
