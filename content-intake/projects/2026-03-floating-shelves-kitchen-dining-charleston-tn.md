# Project 2
## Project Title
Floating Shelves for Kitchen and Dining Area

## Service Type
Floating Shelves

## Location
Charleston, TN

## Summary
Built and installed custom floating shelves to add open storage and a warm wood feature to the kitchen/dining space.

## Scope of Work
- Measured wall area and shelf spacing
- Fabricated custom wood shelves
- Installed concealed mounting hardware
- Leveled and secured shelves
- Applied finish to match the room style

## Customer Goal
The client wanted practical storage with a clean, modern look that still felt warm and handcrafted.

## Key Features
- Custom sizing
- Solid visual presence
- Functional display/storage use
- Seamless floating appearance

## Materials
- Solid wood or hardwood-faced material
- Concealed shelf brackets
- Stain or clear finish

## Outcome
Delivered a clean set of floating shelves that added character, storage, and custom appeal to the space.
