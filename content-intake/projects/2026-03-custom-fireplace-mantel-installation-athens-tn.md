# Project 3
## Project Title
Custom Fireplace Mantel Installation

## Service Type
Mantels

## Location
Athens, TN

## Summary
Designed and installed a custom mantel to upgrade the fireplace and create a stronger visual centerpiece in the room.

## Scope of Work
- Reviewed fireplace dimensions and room style
- Built a custom mantel to fit the opening and wall proportions
- Installed and secured mantel assembly
- Finished and detailed visible surfaces
- Completed final touch-up and fit adjustments

## Customer Goal
The homeowner wanted a more substantial and custom fireplace feature that fit the style of the home.

## Key Features
- Custom-built mantel profile
- Tailored sizing
- Strong centerpiece effect
- Finished carpentry detailing

## Materials
- Solid wood or mantel-grade lumber
- Trim details as needed
- Stain or paint finish

## Outcome
The fireplace became a stronger design feature and gave the room a more finished, custom look.
